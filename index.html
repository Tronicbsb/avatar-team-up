📖 Quick Game Guide (Based on Official Rules)

⚙️ Enemy Turn Structure:

  1. Untap Step: Untap all creatures and bosses the enemy army controls.
  2. Action Phase: Draw the top two cards of the Event Deck sequentially. Reveal the first, resolve it completely (if it is a Tactic, execute and discard; if it is a Creature, put it on the battlefield), and then reveal and resolve the second.
    • Multiplayer Scaling: If playing with 3 or 4 players, reveal 3 cards each action turn!
  3. Combat Step: Each creature in the enemy army attacks the heroes if able (creatures cast this turn do not attack unless they have haste).

🎯 Stage Advancement:

  • When the Boss team's total life reaches 0, that stage is defeated.
  • Remove the old bosses from the game. Keep the minions, the Event Deck, the graveyard, and the turn phase exactly as they are!
  • Reveal the new bosses for the next stage and restore the Bosses' total life (20 HP, or 30 HP if playing with 4 players).
  • Distribution by Stage:
    • 2 or 3 Players (6 Bosses total): Stage 1 = 1 Boss, Stage 2 = 2 Bosses, Stage 3 = 3 Bosses.
    • 4 Players (10 Bosses total): Stage 1 = 2 Bosses, Stage 2 = 3 Bosses, Stage 3 = 5 Bosses.
